SPANISH RICE

Spanish rice

classic Italian dish: long, thin pasta (spaghetti) paired with savory meatballs, often bathed in rich
tomato sauce. comforting and flavorful.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 tablespoons chopped oil
  • 1 1/2 cups uncooked white rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup picante sauce

    • STEPS

    1. Heat oil in a large, heavy skillet over medium heat. Add onion; cook and stir until tender, about 5 minutes.
    2. Add rice; cook and stir until rice begins to turn golden brown. stir in chicken broth and picante sauce.
      Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until liquid has been absorbed, about 15 to 20 minutes.
